Animation undergraduate from Cal State Los Angeles

About me

I am pursuing a B.A. in Animation at California State University, Los Angeles, following my completion of A.A. degrees in Studio Arts, Art History, and Liberal Arts at Bakersfield College. My experience includes working with 3D animation and stop motion techniques.

My skills

  • Video Production: Proficient in video editing, adept at cutting, adding clips, effects, and sound to enhance visual storytelling
  • 3D Animation: Skilled in character posing, in-betweening, and lip-syncing to bring characters and scenes to life.
  • 2D Animation: Experienced in frame-by-frame drawing and coloring for detailed and expressive animations.
  • Stop Motion: Created and utilized custom Stop Motion Puppets and paper puppets to produce compelling animated sequences.
